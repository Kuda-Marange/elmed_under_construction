Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

ELMED Online Academy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website and sign up for launch notifications.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up for launch notifications, we collect only the information you voluntarily provide:

  • Email address — used solely to send you a one-time notification when our platform launches.

We do not collect your name, phone number, address, payment information, or any other personal data through this signup form.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email address for the following purposes only:

  • To send you a single launch notification email when ELMED Online Academy goes live.
  • To respond to any inquiries you may send us via the contact details provided on our site.

We will not use your email for marketing newsletters, promotional campaigns, or any purpose other than what you have consented to.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your email address is stored securely through Formspree, a third-party form processing service. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or alteration.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to process your signup (i.e., Formspree) or as required by law.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your email address only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected — namely, to notify you of our launch. After the launch notification has been sent, we will delete your email address from our records unless you have separately opted into ongoing communications.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct any inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your email address from our records at any time.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.
